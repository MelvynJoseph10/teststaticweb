House Boats:

A houseboat is a huge, slow-moving barges used for leisure trips. Here, freshly caught fish is prepared according to client's preference.
Depending on the customer's wish, the length of the boat stay may change. It's one of the best and most cheap ways to take
in Kerala's backwaters.

Beaches:

God's own country (Kerala) also has a fair set of beaches. Beaches in Kerala may not be as big as the ones in its neighboring states but
still packed with a lot of fun. Tourists mostly spend their time on the beach with freshly cooked fish along with coconut toddy its not only
famous among the tourist but also among the locals.

Munnar (Highly Suggested):

The town and hill resort of Munnar is located in the Idukki district of Kerala, a state in southwest India. Munnar is located in the Western
Ghats mountain range at a height of around 1,600 meters (5,200 feet) above mean sea level. The "Kashmir of South India" is another
name for Munnar, which is also a well-liked honeymoon location.
